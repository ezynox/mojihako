About MojiHako

MojiHako is a small collection of browser-based tools for Japanese text work. You can use tools for full-width and half-width conversion, hiragana and katakana conversion, kana input, genko paper preview, Japanese era conversion, and kaomoji copying directly in your browser.

Contact

For bug reports, feature suggestions, or other questions, please contact us at:

contact@mojihako.com

When reporting a bug or unexpected behavior, including the following information can help with checking the issue.

  • The page where the issue occurred
  • Your browser and device type
  • Example text or steps used
  • Expected result and actual result
  • A screenshot, if available

We cannot guarantee a reply to every message, but bug reports and suggestions may be used to improve the site.
